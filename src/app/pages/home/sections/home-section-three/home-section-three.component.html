Web Development

Web development refers to the process of creating websites and web applications that can be accessed through the internet.It involves designing, developing, and maintaining websites using a combination of programming languages, frameworks, and tools. A web developer typically works with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create the visual design and functionality of a website. Other technologies like databases, server-side scripting, and content management systems may also be used. The goal of web development is to create a user-friendly and visually appealing website that meets the needs of both the business and the users.
